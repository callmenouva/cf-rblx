On Board

Welcome on board our ships

Discover all the services available on Corsica Ferries RBLX ships: dining areas, relaxation spaces, entertainment and much more to make your trip a pleasant and comfortable experience.

Dining

Yellow's: Happy Self Restaurant

A modern and bright self-service restaurant where you can choose between hot dishes, fresh salads, desserts and daily specials. Perfect for a quick but tasty meal, with the cheerful and informal “Go Yellow” style.

Dolcevita

The most elegant restaurant on board: a quiet atmosphere, Mediterranean menu and table service. Ideal for enjoying a quality moment with carefully prepared dishes and a refined experience.

Gusto

The area dedicated to Italian tradition. Here you’ll find freshly baked pizzas, focaccia and delicious hot snacks. Perfect for a simple, fast and flavourful break.

Main Street

The “fast & fresh” heart of the ship: a fast-food style restaurant serving burgers, fries, hot sandwiches, salads and freshly prepared snacks. A lively, informal environment for those who want good food served quickly without giving up classic street-food flavours.

Veranda: All You Can Eat Buffet

A large dining area offering unlimited buffet options: hot dishes, salads, sides, desserts and specialities. The freedom to choose what you want, as much as you want — with a sea view.

Bars and Relax Areas

Lido Beach Bar

A bar located directly on the ship's solarium areas, in full “summer vibes” style, perfect for refreshing yourself during the crossing. Cocktails, chilled drinks, sandwiches and snacks to enjoy outdoors, between sun, relaxation and sea.

Riviera Lounge

An elegant lounge bar where you can relax while enjoying drinks, coffee or aperitifs. Soft music, warm lighting and a relaxing atmosphere for a peaceful break during your journey.

Sweet Café

The ideal space for dessert lovers: cakes, cookies, croissants, ice creams, milkshakes and a full coffee bar. Perfect for a sweet breakfast or a relaxing afternoon treat.

Entertainment

Shops & Duty-Free

Souvenirs, exclusive products and travel items. A small shopping center on board.

Arcade Room

Videogames, mini-arcades and entertainment for younger guests. Guaranteed fun throughout the crossing.

Accommodations

Our cabins will gently take you to your destination.

Standard Cabin

Everything you need for a comfortable journey. Functional and welcoming spaces to rest during the crossing.

Superior Cabin

Larger spaces and additional comforts. Ideal for those seeking an extra touch of luxury during their journey.
